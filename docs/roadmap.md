# Organizational Roadmap

## Purpose

This document outlines the annual strategic priorities for Gladney Pharmaceuticals.

The roadmap defines major organizational initiatives, research milestones, regulatory objectives, technology improvements, and business events that influence employee behavior throughout the year.

Project NAVIDSON uses this roadmap to provide realistic organizational context for synthetic employee activity.

---

# NAVIDSON Relevance

The Organizational Roadmap serves as long-term context for behavioral simulation.

Unlike Organizational Events, which represent day-to-day operational changes, the roadmap establishes the strategic direction of the company over the course of an entire year.

Behavior generated by NAVIDSON should naturally evolve as strategic priorities shift from quarter to quarter.

Examples include:

- Increased research collaboration during conference season.
- Elevated document activity before FDA submissions.
- Increased IT administrative activity during infrastructure upgrades.
- Increased HR workload during hiring cycles.
- Increased executive collaboration during strategic planning.

The roadmap provides another layer of context that helps distinguish expected organizational change from behavior requiring further investigation.
