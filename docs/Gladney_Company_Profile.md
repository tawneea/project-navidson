# Gladney Pharmaceuticals

## Company Overview

Gladney Pharmaceuticals is a fictional mid-sized biopharmaceutical company created as the enterprise environment for PROJECT NAVIDSON.

The company exists solely as a realistic organizational simulation used to model employee behavior, enterprise workflows, cybersecurity telemetry, and explainable behavioral analytics. While entirely fictional, Gladney is designed to reflect the structure, operations, and security challenges of a modern pharmaceutical organization.

Rather than generating isolated cybersecurity events, PROJECT NAVIDSON simulates day-to-day business operations and explores how employee behavior evolves over time in response to organizational context.

---

# Mission

Gladney Pharmaceuticals develops innovative therapies for complex neurological and psychiatric disorders through evidence-based research, rigorous clinical development, and regulatory excellence.

The company's lead investigational compound, **GP-217**, is currently progressing through Phase III clinical development as part of **HELIX**, Gladney's flagship research initiative.

# Organizational Philosophy

Gladney Pharmaceuticals recognizes that employee behavior naturally changes over time.

Business travel, research milestones, software deployments, regulatory deadlines, promotions, onboarding, and organizational events all influence expected behavior.

For this reason, unusual behavior is not automatically considered suspicious.

Behavior should always be interpreted within its organizational context.

---

# Relationship to PROJECT NAVIDSON

Gladney Pharmaceuticals serves as the simulated enterprise environment for **PROJECT NAVIDSON**.

Employees generate realistic synthetic activity across enterprise systems according to their behavioral archetypes, historical baselines, and current organizational context.

Rather than attempting to identify malicious employees, NAVIDSON asks three questions:

1. What changed?
2. What organizational context helps explain the change?
3. Does the accumulated behavioral evidence justify further investigation?

This human-centered approach emphasizes context, explainability, and confidence accumulation over binary classification.

---

# Design Principles

Gladney Pharmaceuticals was intentionally designed to generate realistic enterprise telemetry while preserving privacy.

No real employees, organizations, patients, proprietary datasets, or clinical information are used.

All people, systems, events, and data are synthetic and exist solely for education, research, and software engineering.
