# Gladney Pharmaceuticals

## Company Overview

Gladney Pharmaceuticals is a fictional mid-sized pharmaceutical company created as the organizational environment for Project NAVIDSON.

The company exists solely as a realistic simulation used to model employee behavior, cybersecurity telemetry, and explainable anomaly detection. Although fictional, Gladney is designed to reflect the structure, workflows, and operational characteristics of a modern pharmaceutical organization.

Rather than generating random cybersecurity events, Project NAVIDSON simulates normal business operations and studies how employee behavior changes over time in response to organizational activity.

---

# Mission

Gladney Pharmaceuticals develops innovative therapies addressing complex neurological and psychiatric disorders through evidence-based research, rigorous clinical development, and regulatory excellence.

The company's flagship research program focuses on DYLAR-X, an investigational therapy exploring new approaches to anxiety disorders, post-traumatic stress disorder (PTSD), and treatment-resistant anxiety.

# Organizational Philosophy

Gladney Pharmaceuticals recognizes that employee behavior naturally changes over time.

Factors such as business travel, research milestones, software deployments, regulatory deadlines, promotions, onboarding, and organizational events all influence normal employee activity.

For this reason, unusual behavior is not automatically considered suspicious.

Understanding organizational context is considered essential before drawing security conclusions.

---

# Relationship to Project NAVIDSON

Gladney Pharmaceuticals serves as the simulated enterprise environment for Project NAVIDSON.

Employees generate realistic synthetic activity across enterprise systems based on their behavioral archetypes, historical baselines, and current organizational context.

NAVIDSON observes these behaviors to model explainable behavioral anomaly detection.

Rather than attempting to identify malicious employees, NAVIDSON asks three questions:

1. What changed?
2. What organizational context explains the change?
3. Does the accumulated evidence justify further investigation?

This human-centered approach emphasizes context, transparency, and confidence accumulation over binary classification.

---

# Design Principles

Gladney Pharmaceuticals was intentionally designed to produce realistic cybersecurity telemetry while protecting privacy.

No real employees, organizations, patients, or proprietary datasets are used.

All people, systems, events, and data are synthetic and exist solely for research, education, and software development.
